GST Billing Software

This can be used by Business man who have to create gst invoice and manage all the datas without any paper-work but can't afford costly Software. It is very cheap and efficient to use with good UI/UX.

Login Page

First you have to register yourself and login validation is done using MySQL Database

Sales Manager

This page helps you to create a gst bill for your Sales and bill generated will be saved as a Word Document.

Purchase Manager

Here you can do your data entry of your Purchase if any and data will be saved as an Excel file

Invoices

You can have all the details at a glance of your sales and double tap to open for re-check and if some mistake found you can just edit also. The file can be converted and saved as PDF and you can also print the file

Party Detail

Here you can add your fixed parties detail

Monthly Analysis

Get a monthy analysis of your sales and purchase and save it to Excel including GST Calculation
